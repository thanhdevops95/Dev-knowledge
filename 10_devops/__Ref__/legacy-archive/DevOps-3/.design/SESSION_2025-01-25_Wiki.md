## 🎯 DESIGN DECISIONS MADE

### Decision 005: "Not Afraid of Length"

**Date:** 2025-01-25  
**Decision:** Module 01 README = 40 pages (vs typical 10-15)  
**Rationale:** Comprehensive > concise for learning. "More information is better"  
**Result:** Students won't need external resources

### Decision 006: Real Production Stories

**Date:** 2025-01-25  
**Decision:** Start each module với real incident story  
**Rationale:** Context > theoretical intro. Shows WHY before HOW  
**Impact:** Engages learners, shows real-world relevance

### Decision 007: Progressive Hints in Scenarios

**Date:** 2025-01-25  
**Decision:** Scenarios có collapsible hints (not full solutions inline)  
**Rationale:** Encourage problem-solving, prevent copy-paste  
**Impact:** Better learning outcomes
